FAQ

1. What is Dad, Put Me In The Game?
"Dad, Put Me In The Game" provides personalized and original sports themed children stories. Each story includes the latest league and team sports headlines based upon your personal interest. If you're a Dallas Cowboys fan then your child's story will include the latest NFL and Cowboys news, and if you're a Los Angeles Lakers fan then you'll enjoy the latest NBA and Lakers news. "Dad, Put Me In The Game" provides stories for all National Football League (NFL), National Basketball Association (NBA), Major League Baseball (MLB) and National Hockey League (NHL) teams.
2. Why do I have to enter a name and city in order to begin each story?
Each story is personalized for your child so we request a name and city value be entered in order to be shown in the story. We don't collect any of the information you enter and you can enter any personal name and any city name. It just helps to make the story more personalized for your child.
3. Can you add more leagues?
Yes, we will be adding more leagues in future releases. We are presently working on adding football (soccer).
4. Affiliations?
We are not affilliated or associated with either the National Football League, Major League Baseball, National Hockey League, National Basketball Association or any of their teams.
